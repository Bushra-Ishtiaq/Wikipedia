Science (disambiguation) Science (from the Latin word scientia, meaning "knowledge")is a systematic enterprise that builds and organizes Knowledge in the form of testable the form of testable and predictions about the universe. Science is based on research, which is commonly conducted in academic and research institution and government agencies and companies. The practical impact of scientific research has led to the emergence of science policies that seek to influence the scientific enterprise by prioritizing the development of commerical products, armaments, health care and environmental protection
